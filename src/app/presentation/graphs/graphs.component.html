Pricing Details

  • SanerNow: The plans are based on the number of endpoints to be managed. The Standard plan starts at $2.5 per endpoint per year.
  • Intruder: Intruder offers a range of plans based on the number of assets to be scanned. The Essentials plan starts at $80 per month for up to 10 assets.
  • ManageEngine Vulnerability Manager Plus: A company would need to purchase the Professional plan for up to 250 endpoints, which costs $795 per year.
  • Nessus: A company would need to purchase the Nessus Professional plan for up to 128 IPs, which costs $2,190 per year. (Not limited to the number of assets)
  • Qualys: A company would need to purchase the Express plan for up to 100 assets, which costs $1,995 per year.
